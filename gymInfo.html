Weekly Gym Workout Plan

  • Monday-Chest Day
  • Tuesday-Back Day
  • Wednesday-Shoulder Day
  • Thursday-Biceps Day
  • Friday-Leg Day
  • Saturday-Triceps+Cardio/Abs

Monday-Chest Day

  1. Barbell Bench Press-4 Sets of 8 Reps
  2. Incline Dumbbell Press-4 Sets of 10 Reps
  3. Cable Chest Fly-4 Sets of 12 Reps
  4. Push Up-3 Sets of 15 Reps
  5. Chest Day-3 Sets of 10 Reps
  6. Machine Chest Press-3 Sets of 12 Reps

Tuesday-Back Day

  1. Deadlift-4 Sets of 8 Reps
  2. Pullups-4 Sets of 6 Reps
  3. Barbell Rows-4 Sets of 10 Reps
  4. Seated Cable Rows-3 Sets of 10 Reps
  5. Lat Pulldown-3 Sets of 10 Reps
  6. Hyperextensions-3 Sets of 12 Reps

Wednesday-Shoulder Day

  1. Overhead Barbell Press-4 Sets of 8 Reps
  2. Dumbbell Lateral Raises-3 Sets of 12 Reps
  3. Arnold Press-4 Sets of 10 Reps
  4. Cable Lateral Raises-3 Sets of 12 Reps
  5. Front Dumbbell Raises-3 Sets of 10 Reps
  6. Machine Shoulder Press-3 Sets of 12 Reps

Thursday-Biceps Day

  1. Barbell Curl-4 Sets of 10 Reps
  2. Incline Dumbbell Curl-4 Sets of 12 Reps
  3. Preacher Curl-3 Sets of 10 Reps
  4. Hammer Curl-3 Sets of 10 Reps
  5. Concentration Curl-3 Sets of 12 Reps
  6. Cable Curl-3 Sets of 15 Reps

Friday-Leg Day

  1. Squats-4 Sets of 10 Reps
  2. Leg Press-4 Sets of 12 Reps
  3. Leg Curls-3 Sets of 10 Reps
  4. Seated Calf Raises-3 Sets of 15 Reps
  5. Standing Calf Raises-3 Sets of 20 Reps
  6. Box Jumps-3 Sets of 10 Reps

Saturday-Triceps+Cardio/Abs

  1. Squats-4 Sets 8 Reps
  2. Tricep Dips-3 Sets of 10 Reps
  3. Skull Crushers-3 Sets of 10 Reps
  4. Cable Tricep Extensions-3 Sets of 12 Reps
  5. Push Up-3 Sets of 15 Reps
  6. Planks-3 Sets of 60 Seconds
